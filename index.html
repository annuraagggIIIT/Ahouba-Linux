Ahouba Linux

Ahouba-Linux is a groundbreaking project that aims to revolutionize the way we think about airspace ownership and environmental responsibility. By creating unique Airspace NFTs, this project provides a platform for drone and flight operators to compensate landowners for the carbon footprint emissions generated by their operations.

This innovative approach not only incentivizes environmentally friendly practices but also establishes a tangible link between airspace usage and environmental impact. Through the use of blockchain technology, the compensation process is transparent, secure, and easily auditable, ensuring that landowners are fairly compensated for their contribution to reducing carbon emissions.

Join us in creating a sustainable future where every flight contributes to a cleaner, greener world.
